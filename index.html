OUR STORY

Town Dropped

Based on the idea of having a local citizen to tell visitors his hometown stories, TownDropped appears to fullfil the necessity of keeping and delivering new experiences to every traveller.

Creating the best of the two worlds, TownDropped starts as a project to create sympathy and networking among young people from different countries around the world.
